# **App Name**: Playmakers

## Core Features:

- User Authentication & Profile: Secure sign-up, login, and user profile management to track progress, contests, and friends.
- Multi-Sport Event Browsing: View upcoming and live professional sporting events across various sports (basketball, football, golf, NASCAR, etc.) with essential details.
- Athlete Roster & Stats Viewer: Access curated lists of professional athletes for each sport, with simplified, key performance statistics relevant for pick-em contests.
- Friend-to-Friend Contest Creation: Users can create private, invite-only sweepstakes or pick-em contests for specific sports events and challenge their friends.
- Real-time Score & Standings: Track the live and final performance of selected athletes, automatically updating contest scores and friend rankings within the app.
- Personal Leaderboards: Display leaderboards for each user's private contests, showing rankings among friends.
- In-App Currency & Redemption System: Implement a secure banking system allowing users to purchase 'Gold Coins' for gameplay and redeem 'Sweepstakes Coins' for cash prizes or packages.
- Legal & Sweeps Policy Pages: Develop comprehensive policy pages (e.g., Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, Sweeps Rules) including details for mail-in entry to receive free Sweepstakes Coins.

## Style Guidelines:

- The primary interface will use a dark color scheme, inspired by the competitive energy of sports and a sleek, modern 'arcade game' feel. Key actions and primary information will feature a vibrant royal blue (#3C71DD).
- The main background will be a dark, desaturated blue-grey (#20262C), providing a strong contrast and enhancing readability for event and athlete data.
- A bright, energetic cyan (#54DEFF) will serve as an accent color for interactive elements, highlights, and calls-to-action, drawing attention without being overwhelming.
- Headlines and prominent text will use 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, slightly techy, and engaging feel. Body text and statistical information will use 'Inter' (sans-serif) for excellent legibility and a clean, objective appearance.
- Icons will be modern, sharp, and sport-themed, using clear vector graphics. They should communicate function instantly, such as sport type, challenge status, and leaderboards.
- A responsive, card-based layout will be employed to elegantly display multi-sport events, athlete profiles, and ongoing contests. Focus will be on clear hierarchy and easy navigation, especially on smaller screens.
- Subtle, smooth transitions will enhance the user experience, particularly when navigating between sports, filtering events, or viewing contest results, giving a polished feel.
